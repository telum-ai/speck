# Sprint PRD

**Play Level**: Sprint
**Project**: [Project Name]
**Created**: [Date]
**Kill Date**: [Date — max 2 weeks from created]

---

## What Is It

[2-3 sentences maximum. What does this do? Who is it for? Why does it matter right now?]

## Revenue Model

[One sentence. How does this make money, or why is it worth building for free?]

## Success Metric

**The one number that tells us if this worked:**

> [Metric] reaches [Target] by [Kill Date]

Examples: "50 signups in 48 hours", "10 paying customers in 1 week", "$500 revenue in 2 weeks"

## Kill Criteria

**We stop and move on if:**

- [Specific, measurable kill condition — e.g., "fewer than 10 signups after launch"]

No negotiating. If we hit this, we kill it.

## Build Plan

1. [First thing to build — must be shippable alone]
2. [Second thing — only if #1 works]
3. [Third thing — only if #2 warrants it]

Maximum 3 bullets. If you need more, it's not a Sprint project.

---

*Sprint projects live or die in days, not months. Ship it, measure it, decide.*
